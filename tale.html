The Text And LanguagE (TALE) Group

The Text and LanguagE (TALE) group work on machine learning for real-world applications, with a focus on natural language processing and textual data analysis. The group has PhD students, Masters students and Honours students working on topics such as sentiment analysis and opinion mining, misinformation detection and mitigation and digital health.
